Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our application (“the App”) handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Offline-Only Operation

The App operates entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection for its core functionality and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Collection

No Data Collected:
The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data or usage information. No information is gathered about you, your device, or your usage of the App.

4. Data Sharing

No Data Shared:
Since the App does not collect any data, there is no information to share with third parties, service providers, or affiliates.

5. Data Deletion

No Data to Delete:
As the App does not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to delete. You do not need to make any requests for data deletion.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any information from users, including children under the age of 13, and is compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

7. Compliance with Laws

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other relevant data protection laws. Since no data is collected, most requirements of these laws do not apply.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our website, with the updated date indicated above.
